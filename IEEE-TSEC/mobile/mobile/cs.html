IEEE Computer Society


With nearly 85,000 members worldwide, the IEEE Computer Society is the world’s leading organization of computing professionals. The Computer Society serves the information and career-development needs of today’s computing researchers and professionals with books, conferences, conference publications, magazines, online courses, software development certifications, standards, and technical journals. The Society offers a rich repository of online content and tools, from Computing Now, which provides free articles and multimedia content, to the Build Your Career career-development site, and the e-Learning Campus, where members can access more than 3,000 online courses and 600 online books.

Mission:

The Society shall strive to advance the theory, practice, and application of computer and information processing science and technology and shall maintain a high professional standard among its members.
To be essential to the global technical community and computer professionals everywhere and be universally recognized for the contributions of technical professionals in developing and applying technology to improve global conditions, is the main aim of the Computer Society.

Vision:

To be the leading provider of technical information, community services, and personalized services to the world’s computing professionals.

IEEE-TSEC Computer Society

IEEE-TSEC Computer Society offers myriad benefits for individuals to develop a cumulative interest in the field of computing technology.

IEEE-TSEC Computer Society membership offers various advantages like :

  • Annual placements.
  • Free access to the Digital Library and several online courses.
  • Latest softwares.
  • IEEE-TSEC Computer Society offers the prestigious Richard E. Merwin scholarship, which ranges across a few thousand dollars.

A multitude of interactive workshops, seminars and conferences are held throughout the year, for the benefit of its members.

Events

IEEE-TSEC Computer Society holds two festivals during the course of one academic year: MOSAIC, during the even semester, and IGNITION during the odd semester.

MOSAIC

MOSAIC is an intra-collegiate fest held during the month of February. It consists of conferences and workshops held by professionals from leading companies and technical institutes. This fest aims to expand the technical knowledge of students participating in the fest, by actively engaging them in the workshops and seminars.

MOSAIC 2015

MOSAIC 2015 was held on 21st February.
It comprised of a workshop on Cinema 4D.

MOSAIC 2012 was held on 21st and 22nd February.

  • It comprised of a workshop on VFX (Visual Effects)
  • It comprised of a workshop on CorelDraw, taken by Arena Animation.

IGNITION

IGNITION is an intra-collegiate fest held during the month of August. IGNITION usually consists of workshops and fun events, which test the managerial and organizational skills of the members and the volunteers.

ANNUAL PLACEMENTS

Annual Placements have always been a stronghold of IEEE-TSEC COMPUTER SOCIETY. Members of the society are placed in different companies for internships related to computing. The placed members work on a project given to them by the company and their goal is to complete it within a given period. In return, the Company awards the members with a Letter of Appreciation, which adds to their resume. Members submit their resumes to the committee and the committee places them at the companies according to their qualification. Thus, the committee ensures that the name of the Society is upheld within leading companies of Mumbai.
