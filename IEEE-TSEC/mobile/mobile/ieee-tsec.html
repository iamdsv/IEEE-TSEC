Thadomal Shahani Engineering College(TSEC)

Established in 1983, Thadomal Shahani Engineering College is founded by the Hyderabad (Sind) National Collegiate Board. Thadomal Shahani Engineering College (TSEC) is recognized by the Government of Maharashtra. This college is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Affiliated to the University of Mumbai, TSEC is graded as an 'A' grade educational institution. It offers a 4-year Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) Degree program in Information Technology, Computer Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Biomedical Engineering, Biotechnology and Chemical Engineering. The College also offers instructions at the M.E. level in Computer Engineering (25 seats).

Students excel in their academics and are known to hold rank in the University. In addition to academic excellence students are known as able sportspersons, who have represented the College at several inter and intra state Colleges. The students have represented the college in the National Team of Badminton, Swimming and Rifle Shooting. Since 1995, TSEC has been hosting the 'Inter-Engineering Football tournament' which presents Rotating Trophies to Winner and Runner-up teams. TSEC has been hosting the 'Inter-Engineering Cultural Festival' TRIFLES since academic year 2000, which presents a platform for students from different colleges to interact with one another.

The College has its own Management Society (MS), which conducts students' activities and prepares them for various aspects of management including Mock GRE program for Engineering Graduates from Mumbai aspiring for Post-BE Engineering studies in USA. .

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) USA, an International Professional Organization, has established a student's chapter in the college which has 4 Societies chapters viz. Computer Society Chapter, Communications Society Chapter, Engineering in Medicine & Biology Society Chapter and Industrial Electronics Society Chapter

IEEE-TSEC

The IEEE Student Branch of Thadomal Shahani Engineering College is renowned in Region 10 (Asia-Pacific) for its efforts to make available the latest technologies, resources and services to its members. It was inaugurated by the ex-principal Prof. Kranti Kumar in 1990. Since then IEEE-TSEC aims at helping its members enhance their technical knowledge and build their careers by widening the scope of engineering. IEEE-TSEC is one of the largest student branches of IEEE which provides its members with co-curricular technical know-how within th e college. It helps in inducing the latest technological advancements and imbibes a zealous participation among its members. We, at IEEE-TSEC, aim at raising technical awareness among our members through a variety of workshops and seminars along with industrial visits to gain an insight into the professional world.

IEEE-TSEC is the largest student body of IEEE in Mumbai. It helps in spreading knowledge about the latest technological advancements and imbibes a zealous participation among its members. IEEE-TSEC encourages its members to keep an open mind and instills in them a will to excel. Curiosity is one of the most important traits of genius and we at IEEE-TSEC aim at feeding our member's curiosities by encouraging them to explore discover and learn. IEEE also has four of its Chapters active in TSEC, namely Computer Society, Communication Society, Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society and Industry Applications Society. The events organized by this committee give immense exposure to new technological advancements and keeps students abreast of the latest technical happenings.
