About ISAAC

IEEE-TSEC Student Activities and Conferences (ISAAC) focuses on inquisitiveness, perseverance and penchant for hard work through fusion of knowledge, experimentation and analysis. It works at encouraging the creativity of the future engineers. Being the annual inter-collegiate technical festival of IEEE-TSEC, ISAAC continues bringing out the best in students through seminars, workshops and contests for over 14 years. ISAAC proudly holds the title of being the largest technical festival under Mumbai University. It is an arena, a platform for young intellectual minds to learn and imbibe technical knowledge, battle in contests honing their creative and technical skills, and win laurels commending their perseverance.

ISAAC is a huge endeavour, comprising of top level conferences, technical workshops, contests and exhibitions each year. Each year, the committee strives to exceed expectations, and make ISAAC bigger and better than ever. .

ISAAC 2014 was a magnificent enterprise, having a plethora of conferences, seminars, workshops and technical contests. With the number of participants and spectators rising to SEVERAL HUNDREDS, ISAAC 2014 was a resounding success!

CONFERENCES

Every year, ISAAC holds major conferences for the benefit of the students. These conferences are conducted by eminent names in the industrial world, which come to our college to carry out the conference. The conferences revolve around prominent and up-to-date technologies and futuristic ideas, to acquaint students about modern and latest developments in technology.
ISAAC 2014 conducted Conferences on:

1. Software as a service

Conducted by: - Mr. Shrikant Mulik, L & T InfoTech. L & T InfoTech conducted a conference on “Software as a service”. The students learnt how softwares are delivered through internet i.e. instead of installing software how one can access it online.

2. Forensics

Conducted by:- Mr. Sandeepan Mukherjee, Scientific Officer, Department of Virology & Immunology, Haffkine Institute Experts from the Haffkine Institute conducted a conference on the topic “Forensics” where the students learnt the use of forensic science in various fields like criminal, archaeology, pathology, etc.

3. Free and Open Source Software

Conducted by: - Mr. Abhishek Potnis and his team including Mr. Akshat Kedia and Mr. Sushant Hiray, Volunteering at Mozilla India Professionals from Mozilla India conducted a conference on “Free and Open Source Software” where the students acquired detailed knowledge about Open Source Coding and learnt how to create webpages using Mozilla Firefox Browser.

EXIBITION

Through exhibitions we try to showcase the technology to the future generation, giving a wide exposure to technology.
